20–30% of mushrooms lost every year to overwatering & spoilage

The Problem

Across Asia, mushroom farms still rely on manual monitoring. Workers cannot watch every shelf around the clock, so mature mushrooms get over-watered and rot — a severe, avoidable harvest loss for smallholder farms in Binh Duong, Vietnam.

Our Solution — a self-driving watering system

📷
AI Camera
160° camera on a moving rod scans each shelf
›
🧠
YOLOv8 Vision
Detects growth stage — decides whether to water
›
🌡️
Climate AI
Temp + humidity model decides when to water
›
💧
Auto Irrigation
IoT valve waters only when it helps
›
📊
Dashboard
Live monitoring & timing for the farmer
Two cooperating AI models: a vision model (what is on the shelf) and a climate model (the right moment to water) — with hard safety guardrails.
